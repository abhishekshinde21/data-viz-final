How Manchester City showed they are the top-flight team in the Premier League

In the Premier League 2020-2021 season, Manchester City (MC) finished at the top of the standings with an exemplary record of 86 points, consisting of 27 wins, five draws, and six losses. This was 12 points higher than the second place team, Manchester United, which is really impressive. Throughout the season, it was clear that MC would prosper with their football intelligence, possession-focused style of play, and talented squad in both offensive and defensive ends.
