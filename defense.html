Defense: Measuring Clean Sheets and Goals Allowed

  • Please hover over each circle marker in the plot to get more information in the form of a tooltip.

Defense is critical for preventing opponents from scoring. In football, the goalkeeper and defenders are essential for a defense composition. The saying goes, "The best offense is a good defense". Well, Manchester City exemplified the best defense along with the offense this past year.


The team had the highest clean sheet percentage of 50.0, meaning that they had the most games in which they prevented the opponent from scoring at all. This measure enabled the goalkeeper to win the Golden Glove award. In addition, the team allowed only 32 goals throughout the season, which was the least. This defensive superiority inevitably contributed towards their top placement in the league. Overall, from all of the charts, we can see how Manchester City was the strongest side of the 2020-2021 season in the Premier League.
