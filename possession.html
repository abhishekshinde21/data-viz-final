Possession (%) by Each Premier League Team

  • Please hover over each bar marker in the plot to get more information in the form of a central tooltip.

Possession is the percentage of the total successful passes in a game. Manchester City (MC) is known for the "tiki-taka" style of play, in which they control the game with accurate short passes and regaining control of the ball if lost. This allows the team to keep composure and potentially frustrate opponents; in addition, having this technical ability promotes scoring goals from optimal options, alleviating players from running as much, and regrouping from opponent presses.


As the chart indicates, MC led the league in possession, with 63.9% followed by Liverpool with 62.4%. Examining MC's tooltip, the team's players also averaged the highest progressive distance, which is the total yards the players controlled the ball as they approached the opponent's goal.
